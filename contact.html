Quick Answers

Common Questions

How quickly will you respond to my message?
+
We aim to reply to all email enquiries within 6 hours. For urgent matters, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us — we're available 24/7.
Can I speak to a scholar directly about my enquiry?
+
Yes. For Shariah-related questions about Badal, we can arrange a consultation with one of our qualified Ulama via WhatsApp or phone call. Select "Shariah Question" in the subject field.
I've already booked a service — how do I follow up?
+
For existing bookings, select "Existing Booking" in the Subject field and provide your booking reference number or the name used at booking. We'll update you within 3 hours.
Do you accept bookings by phone or WhatsApp only?
+
You can book through our website, via WhatsApp, or through email. All bookings are confirmed with a written receipt and a unique booking reference number.
