Our Story

Born from Compassion & Faith

"We began with a single Umrah performed for a widow's late husband — and a promise that no Muslim should be deprived of this mercy."

Umratul Badal was founded by a group of Islamic scholars residing in Makkah al-Mukarramah who witnessed first-hand the grief of families unable to perform Hajj or Umrah for their loved ones. The need was clear — a trustworthy, Shariah-certified service that brought peace of mind to Muslims around the world.

What began as a small grassroots effort has grown into a globally trusted organisation, serving Muslims in over 40 countries across six continents. Every service we offer is rooted in authentic Islamic knowledge, transparent documentation, and a genuine desire to serve the Ummah.

وَمَا تُقَدِّمُوا لِأَنفُسِكُم مِّنْ خَيْرٍ تَجِدُوهُ عِندَ اللَّهِ

"Whatever good you put forward for yourselves, you will find it with Allah." — Quran 2:110

Today, Umratul Badal stands as a registered organisation with a physical office in London, operational scholars in Makkah and Madinah, and a team dedicated to excellence in every service we provide — from booking to proof of completion.

Purpose & Direction

Our Mission & Vision

🕌 Our Mission

To Fulfil Sacred Obligations

مهمتنا

Our mission is to make the sacred rites of Umrah and Hajj accessible to every Muslim — regardless of their physical ability or geographic location. We provide Shariah-certified proxy services performed by qualified scholars in the Holy Cities, ensuring that no Muslim is deprived of this spiritual mercy. We uphold the highest standards of Islamic scholarship, transparency, and trust in everything we do.

🌍 Our Vision

A World United in Worship

رؤيتنا

We envision a world where every Muslim — wherever they are — can fulfil their religious duties and those of their loved ones with complete confidence and peace. We aim to be the world's most trusted Islamic proxy services provider, bridging the distance between Muslims and the Baitullah through scholarship, technology, and unwavering dedication to the service of the Ummah.

Our Journey

The Umratul Badal Story

From a humble beginning in Makkah to serving Muslims in 40+ countries

2018 · Founded

The First Umrah Badal

Sheikh Ibrahim performed the first Umrah Badal for a widow's late husband in Makkah. The family wept with gratitude — and Umratul Badal was born.

🌱
2019 · Growth

First 100 Services Completed

Word spread among Muslim communities in the UK, USA, and Malaysia. We completed our first 100 Umrah Badal services and issued our first digital certificates.

📜
2020 · Expansion

Hajj Badal & Madinah Services

We expanded to offer full Hajj Badal services and personal Dua at the Rawdah in Madinah. Sheikh Yusuf joined the team as our Madinah scholar.

🕋
2021 · Official Registration

Registered in the United Kingdom

Umratul Badal officially registered in the United Kingdom, establishing our London office and formalising our operations, documentation, and compliance processes.

🏛️
2023 · 40+ Countries

Global Community

We reached 40+ countries and launched multilingual support in Arabic, English, Urdu, French, Malay, and more. Our team grew to 12 scholars and support staff.

🌍
2025 · Today

2,500+ Umrahs & Growing

We've now completed over 2,500 Umrahs, 340 Hajj Badals, and issued 1,000+ certificates. The journey continues — one sacred service at a time.
